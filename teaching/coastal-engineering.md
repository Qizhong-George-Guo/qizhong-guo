This page provides course materials for the graduate course **Coastal Engineering**, including the course outline, lecture slide decks, and selected supporting resources. Materials are organized by topic and updated periodically.

---

## Course Information

- **Course level:** Graduate  
- **Focus:** Coastal processes, wave mechanics, sediment transport, shoreline change, and engineering design applications  
- **Audience:** Graduate students in civil, environmental, and coastal engineering

---

## Course Outline

- Introduction to coastal systems and processes  
- Linear and nonlinear wave theory  
- Wave transformation: shoaling, refraction, diffraction, breaking  
- Coastal sediment transport  
- Shoreline change and coastal morphology  
- Coastal structures and protection measures  
- Sea-level rise and coastal resilience

*(Outline may evolve over time.)*
